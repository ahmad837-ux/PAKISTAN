PDM

Pakistan Democratic Movement

PRESIDENT

Fazal-ur-Rehman (politician)

The Pakistan Democratic Movement or PDM, is a conservative political movement of political parties in Pakistan against the elected government of Prime Minister Imran Khan. It is led by Islamic extremist Maulana Fazal-ur-Rehman, the leader of the Islamist Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam, and supported by the two parties who have previously led Pakistani politics, the Pakistan Muslim League (N) and the Pakistan Peoples Party.
